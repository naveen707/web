Discover Why Choosing Us is the Best Decision

Our Vision is to provide a platform where businesses and individuals can easily find and hire the right freelancer for their project, with minimal hassle and maximum satisfaction. We believe in the power of freelancing to provide more opportunities and flexibility for workers, and we want to be at the forefront of this revolution by delivering outstanding results to both clients and freelancers.

Vast Talent Pool

Mercalli Freelance boasts a diverse and extensive talent pool, allowing you to find the perfect freelancer for your specific needs.

Variety of Skills

From web development to graphic design, writing to marketing, Mercalli covers a wide range of skills and expertise, catering to diverse project requirements.

Seamless Hiring Process

Mercalli streamlines the hiring process, making it easy to connect with freelancers, review their profiles, and make informed decisions.

Secure and Trustworthy

With Mercalli, you can trust that your transactions and confidential information are handled securely, ensuring a safe and reliable freelance experience.

Efficient Communication

Mercalli facilitates smooth communication between clients and freelancers through integrated messaging tools, ensuring effective collaboration throughout the project.

Global Reach

Mercalli offers a global platform, providing access to freelancers from around the world, enabling you to tap into international expertise and perspectives.

Community Support

Mercalli fosters a supportive community where clients can connect, share insights, and gain inspiration, creating a vibrant ecosystem for freelancers and clients alike.

Competitive Pricing

Mercalli's freelance marketplace promotes fair pricing, allowing you to find talented freelancers at competitive rates, ensuring value for your investment.

Revenue Model

Mercalli Freelance will generate revenue by charging a commission fee on all completed projects. This fee will be a percentage of the total project cost and will be paid by both the client and freelancer.

Dedicated AI Representative

The website will also offer additional premium services, such as a dedicated personalised AI representative to assist in the vetting process, this will speed up the reporting of potential freelancers for their projects.
